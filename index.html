Installation:

Once you have the QPython 2 app, create a script in the app and copy the contents of this script into it.

Usage:

It will ask for the URL, a filename, and if it is music or a video.
Enter the URL of the YouTube video URL, whatever you want the file to be called, and m for music or v for video.
Everything should be surrounded by quotes ("") when you enter it. This is VERY important.


This is what it should look like when you use it:

YouTube Downloader by Alex M

URL: "https://youtube.com/watch?v=..."
Filename: "The Name you Want"
Music or Video (m/v): "m" (or "v" for videos)
Success
The Name you Want is located at /sdcard/Music (or /sdcard/Download for videos)
