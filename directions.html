Face Wash

NAJI Hydrating Face Wash moisturizes while gently removing dirt, oil, and makeup.

Directions:
Wet your face with warm water by using your fingertips or a damp washcloth, then apply NAJI face wash in circular motions (avoid eyes), then pat dry.


Exfoliant

NAJI Acne Scrub helps prevent future breakouts by exfoliating the dead skin cells and by refining your pores. Good for oily and acne-prone skin because it penetrates the pores and cleans the skin.

Directions:
After using the NAJI Face Wash, ue a damp washcloth in circular motions and rinse thoroughly and pat dry. Only 2-3 times a week.


Moisturizer

NAJI Intense Moisturizing Cream will put all the natural moisture back into your skin.

Directions:
Apply NAJI moisturizer with your fingertips in circular motions evenly across your face.


Lip Balm

NAJI Lip Moisturizer is good for dry lips and to add moisture to your lips.

Directions:
Apply NAJI Lip Moisturizer with an even amount of balm across the lips.


Facial Spray

NAJI Prep & Set Facial Spray can be used to prep your face before makeup and set your face after so it stays all day long.

Directions:
Using NAJI Prep & Set, hold bottle about 3-4 inches away from your face and do a couple sprays around your whole face to prep and set your face.
